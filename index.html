About Us

AMEA Agro and Mining Export Alliance Limited (AMEA) is an integrated agricultural and mining company committed to delivering value across Nigeria and beyond. Established in 2017, AMEA combines cutting-edge technology, industry expertise, and sustainability to drive innovation in mining and agriculture.

From exploration and resource development to integrated agriculture, we deliver exceptional value to investors, communities, and the broader economy.
